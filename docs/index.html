HackX 2K21

INTER UNIVERSITY STARTUP CHALLENGE

Why HackX?

hackX – one of the most successful Inter-University Startup Challenges unfolds its sixth successive chapter as hackX 2021, addressing the innovative minds of the Sri Lankan undergraduates. With the theme of Sustainable Innovation, hackX 2021 Innovative Startup Challenge will not only be limited to tech-based solutions; we are also open to any sustainable eco-friendly idea that will help the planet earth to survive, allowing more solid refined startups to emerge through the industrial level product development guidance provided.

Steps to follow

1

FORM A TEAM AND SUBMIT A PROPOSAL

Form a team with members who shares your idea and submit the proposal

2

PITCH YOUR IDEA IN 5 MINUTES

30 teams from initial proposal will get the opportunity to pitch their idea to our judge panel in 5 minutes.

3

GET SELECTED TO HACKX FINALS

Get selected to finals of hackX 2021 from the Initial - Pitch. Three best teams from the IdeaSprint - Intra department hackathon competition will also be participating in the hackx 2021 finals.

4

ATTEND TO ONLINE WORK- SHOP SERIES

Attend these valuable workshops as they are supposed to give industrial guidance to transfer your idea to a business solution

5

DEVELOP YOUR IDEA INTO A PRODUCT

Develop your idea into a product with the guidance from workshops and with the help of mentors.

6

GRAB THE HACKX 2021 TROPHY

Grab the hackX 2021 trophy and walk away with amazing prizes and experiences as entrepreneurs.

Things to remember

PARTICIPANTS

Undergraduates of any University or Higher Educational Institue are eligible to participate in this competition

THE TEAM

A team must compromise of 3 to 5 members

MEMBERS

Members in a team should represent the same Universityor the Higher Educational Institute

IDEA PRESENTING

Ideas Should be presented in a proposal, following proposal structure and the given guidelines

NOVEL IDEAS

All ideas should be novel and related to Information Technology aspects

PROPOSAL

Proposed idea connot be changed. Final product may have added features.
